ESP Web Flasher

A reliable tool for flashing ESP32 firmware using the Web Serial API.
Works best on Chromium-based browsers (Chrome, Edge) with the correct USB drivers installed.

Instructions:

  1. Connect your ESP32 board to your computer with a **data-capable** USB cable.
  2. **Press and hold** the **BOOT** button on your board.
  3. While holding BOOT, **press and release** the **RESET** button.
  4. **Release** the **BOOT** button.
  5. Your device is now in bootloader mode. Click the "Connect" button below.
