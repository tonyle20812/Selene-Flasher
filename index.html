ESP Web Flasher

A simple, one-shot tool for flashing ESP32 firmware.

Instructions:

  1. **Step 1:** Connect your ESP32 board and put it in bootloader mode (press and hold **BOOT**, then press and release **RESET**, then release **BOOT**).
  2. **Step 2:** Select your firmware file below.
  3. **Step 3:** Click the "Connect and Flash" button.
