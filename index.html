ESP Web Flasher

A simple and robust tool for flashing ESP32 firmware.

Instructions:

  1. **Step 1:** Connect your ESP32 board and press and hold the **BOOT** button.
  2. **Step 2:** Click the "Connect to Device" button.
  3. **Step 3:** The tool will attempt to connect. Once successful, you can release the **BOOT** button.
  4. **Step 4:** Select your firmware file below and click the "Flash Firmware" button.
